Danielle Hinkley for Gold Hill City Council

I want your vote so that I can do what councilors are elected to do and nothing else:

City Communications

Adopting a digital infrastructure with an updated web page and digital footprint will allow for greater transparency in City Hall.

City Hall should adopt a digital infrastructure that utilizes new technologies in order to communicate more effectively with citizens.

Budget

We need to make sure that we are not spending money just because it is available. I will seek to find ways to reduce spending and spend wisely where we have to.

I am a fiscal conservative that believes tax money should be spent only when necessary on maintenance, infrastructure and debt reduction.
